Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how JavaSense - Tech handles user data for the Horos-Koplo Android application.

Horos-Koplo currently operates primarily with data stored locally on the user's device. This policy reflects the current version of the app and must be updated before any future release that adds sign-in, cloud sync, analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or other third-party data processing.

1. Developer and contact information

Developer name: JavaSense - Tech
Support contact: radenraradewianggraini@gmail.com

2. What data the app currently uses

Based on the current app flow, Horos-Koplo may store the following information locally on the user's device to provide app functionality:

  • Selected zodiac sign
  • Display name or nickname entered by the user
  • Daily reading history or cached reading data
  • App preferences such as lightweight settings needed for the app experience

3. How the data is used

The data described above is used only to provide app features, such as personalizing zodiac readings, saving user preferences, caching daily content, and supporting the normal operation of the app.

4. Data sharing

Based on the current version of Horos-Koplo, the app is designed to function without mandatory account creation and without routine transmission of the locally stored reading/profile data to the developer's server.

Horos-Koplo does not state that it sells personal data. If a future version adds third-party services, analytics, ads, cloud storage, or other integrations that access, collect, use, or share user data, this policy will be updated before those changes are released.

5. Data retention

Local app data remains on the user's device until it is removed by the user, such as by clearing app data, using any in-app reset functionality that may be available, or uninstalling the app.

If a user contacts support by email, that correspondence may be retained for a reasonable period needed to respond, troubleshoot, comply with legal obligations, or keep support records.

6. Data deletion

Because the current version of the app primarily stores profile and reading data locally on the device, users can generally remove that data by clearing the app's data in Android settings or uninstalling the app.

For more details, see the Delete Data page.

7. Security

The developer takes reasonable steps to avoid unnecessary collection of data and to keep data handling limited to app functionality. Users should keep their devices protected with appropriate device-level security such as screen locks and system updates.

8. Children's privacy

Horos-Koplo is not specifically directed to children. If you believe personal data from a child has been shared with the developer directly through support contact, please contact radenraradewianggraini@gmail.com.

9. International availability

This app may be made available to users in multiple regions. Users remain responsible for reviewing whether the app and these policy terms are suitable for their jurisdiction and use case.

10. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes should be reflected on this page before the updated handling is relied upon in a release.
